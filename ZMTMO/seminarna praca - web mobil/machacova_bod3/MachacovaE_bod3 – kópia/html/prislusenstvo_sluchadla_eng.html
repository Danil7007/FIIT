Sony MDR-EX155AP, white

The Sony MDR-EX155AP microphone headphone, white, gives you the ultimate in your favorite tracks. With a range of 5 - 24,000 Hz, you can always hear high and low tones completely clear. In addition to the microphone, the Sony headphone cable also has controls for answering or switching audio. This makes these headphones in a subtle white color the perfect companion to travel busy traffic.

In addition, the headphones weigh only three grams and are compact in size - so you can wrap them in the smallest pocket. After being pulled out, they are immediately ready for use as they do not become entangled due to the knurled cable. Headphones with Sony MDR-EX155AP microphone include replaceable SS / S / M / L-size terminals and cable length adjustment clip. Your comfort is thus guaranteed.

Key features of Sony MDR-EX155AP headset, white:

  • Quality sound - 5 - 24,000 Hz range
  • The remote control on the Sony headphones cable allows you to answer a handsfree call
  • Four ear tip sizes: SS / S / M / L
  • The knurled cable does not become entangled
  • The headset only weighs 3g
