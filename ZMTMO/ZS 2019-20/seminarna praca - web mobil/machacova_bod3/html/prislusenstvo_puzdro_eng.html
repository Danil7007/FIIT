Huawei Original Folio Pink pre P20 Lite

This original pink flip case is made of high quality materials and thanks to its successful design, it not only gives your device great protection against damage, but also gives it an exclusive look. Being genuine accessories, easy access to all controls is a matter of course.

In addition, it features a smart view window for easy call acceptance without opening the front cover. When closed, the case automatically dims the display and goes into sleep mode. On opening, the mobile phone is activated.

Key Features of Huawei Original Folio Pink :

  • Elegant design for your unique style
  • Made for Huawei P20 Lite
  • Unlimited access to all buttons
  • Stylish pink finish and quality materials
