Huawei Original Charger CP84 White

Huawei Original Charger CP84 White is an original travel charger that is very powerful. The charging current reaches up to 4 A and it goes without saying that support for quick charging is guaranteed. With this 40 W charger, you can charge other mobile phones or tablets with USB-C connector in addition to Huawei devices. Huawei Original Charger charges up to 70% faster than conventional chargers. Of course there are safety elements in the form of protection against overvoltage or short circuit.

Key Features of Huawei Original Charger CP84 White Travel Charger:

  • Support rapid charging
  • USB-C conector
  • Compact
  • Including cable
  • Power 40 W
  • Charging current 4 A
  • The charging rate is affected by the maximum current indicated in amperes (A) that the charger is able to supply to the device. The higher the current, the faster the charging time.
